About PhishArmor

PhishArmor is a browser extension designed to assist users in detecting potential phishing and spam emails. It analyzes the content of emails and identifies key phrases commonly found in phishing attempts. By alerting users to suspicious emails, PhishArmor helps to protect against fraudulent activities and safeguard sensitive information.
