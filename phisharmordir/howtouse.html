How to Use PhishArmor

This section will provide instructions on how to use the PhishArmor extension.

Steps:

  1. When reading emails, text messages, or any other message, copy the contents of the message.
  2. Open the PhishArmor extension.
  3. Paste the copied content into the extension.
  4. Click the "Test Phishing Likelihood" button to determine the likelihood of spam.
